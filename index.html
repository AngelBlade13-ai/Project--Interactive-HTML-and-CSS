Kingdom Hearts 1

Kingdom Hearts is an action role-playing game developed and published by Square (now Square Enix) for the PlayStation 2. It was released in 2002 as a collaboration between Square and Disney Interactive Studios. The game combines characters and settings from Disney films with those from Square's Final Fantasy series, creating a unique crossover experience.

Kingdom Hearts 2

Kingdom Hearts II is an action role-playing game developed and published by Square Enix for the PlayStation 2. Released in 2005, it is the sequel to the original Kingdom Hearts game. The game continues the story of Sora, Donald Duck, and Goofy as they journey through various Disney-themed worlds while battling the forces of darkness.

Kingdom Hearts 3

Kingdom Hearts III is an action role-playing game developed and published by Square Enix for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. Released in 2019, it is the long-awaited sequel to Kingdom Hearts II and serves as the conclusion to the "Dark Seeker Saga." The game features stunning graphics, new gameplay mechanics, and a wide array of Disney and Pixar worlds for players to explore.
